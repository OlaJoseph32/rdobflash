    About Artiste

    Bright Ogochuckwu Ukpene, also known as Bflash, is not just a Nigerian renowned hip hop artiste, but a true musical sensation. With his unique style and captivating lyrics, he has captured the hearts of music lovers all around the world.

    Growing up in Ore, Ondo State and Ojodu Berger in Lagos State, Bflash love for music was evident from a young age. He would spend hours writing and composing songs, honing his craft and perfecting his art. His dedication and passion for music are what sets him apart from other artists in the industry.

    He gained recognition in 2012 after releasing MY BIRTHDAY, Bflash continued to pursue his musical dreams, releasing hit after hit singles that gained him critical acclaim and numerous awards. His songs are not just about entertainment, but also about spreading positivity and inspiring his listeners to chase their dreams.

    With his upcoming releases, Bflash aims to reach even greater heights in the music industry and solidify his status as one of the top hip hop artistes in the world. Keep an eye out for Bright Ogochuckwu Ukpene, as he is truly a force to be reckoned with in the music world.
